# Hors d'oeuvres & Enhancements Menu

Cocktail-reception bites and enhancements that can be added to any event:
tray-passed and displayed appetizers, dips and displays, action and carving
stations, and desserts. Action stations require a chef on site and carry a $100
station set-up fee.
