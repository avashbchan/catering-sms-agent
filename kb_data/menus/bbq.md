# Barbecue Menu

Casual, crowd-pleasing BBQ for cookouts, company picnics, and relaxed
gatherings. Offered as tiered packages ("little pig" tiers) plus a la carte
sides, desserts, and beverages. Two side dishes come with each package.

## Packages

### 1st Little Pig
- "Great South" BBQ pulled pork - brined and smoked bone-in pork shoulder, spicy house made BBQ sauce [gf]
- Smoked chicken - bone-in or boneless, sweet and tangy house made BBQ sauce [gf]
- Two side dishes (see Sides)
- Buns or rolls
- Choice of seasonal cobbler OR white chocolate banana pudding with toasted coconut topping
