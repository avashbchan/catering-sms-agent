# Buffet Menu

Buffet-style service for weddings, receptions, and large gatherings. For a
balanced menu we suggest a minimum of one salad, one entree, two vegetables,
and one starch. Additional items may be added to enhance the menu, including
appetizers, carving stations, and more. Mixed vegetables are based on seasonal
availability.
