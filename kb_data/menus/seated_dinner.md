# Seated Served Dinner Menu

Formal plated dinners - weddings, galas, and upscale receptions. A plated
appetizer or soup, a salad, an entree, and dessert. Ask about dual entree
portions at a reduced price. A second salad dressing is available tableside for
$0.50 per person.
