# Celebration of Life

Full-service catering and staffing for memorial gatherings and celebrations of
life, in any location - from a private home to a house of worship.

This offering does NOT have a fixed item list. Menus and services are fully
customized to the family's wishes and needs, drawing on any of Talk of the
Town's other menus (brunch, buffet, hors d'oeuvres, seated dinner, etc.). We
handle food and staffing so the family does not have to.

Because everything here is bespoke, guide the customer to reach our events team
directly to build the gathering with the care it deserves:

  Call 770.594.1567 or use the contact page at talkofthetownatlanta.com.

Be warm and unhurried. Offer condolences, gather the basic details (date,
approximate guest count, location, any food preferences), and hand off to staff
for the customized plan and pricing.
